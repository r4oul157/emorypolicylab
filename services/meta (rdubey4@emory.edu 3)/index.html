Systematic Review and Metaanalysis of Unconditional Cash Transfer Studies

Since May 2020, EPL members have systematically searched, collected, categorized, and analyzed the recent but vast literature analyzing the effects of unconditional cash transfers (UCTs) on household welfare.

At present, students are empirically analyzing the characteristics of these studies and their findings in order to draw more general inferences on the state of the literature and the types of programs that have been studied.

This project will result in a full-length manuscript to be submitted to an academic journal by Spring 2021.

Project lead: Karoline Lin

Contributors (current): Raoul Dubey, Sirui Hu, Emlyn Langlieb, Joshua Richter

Contributors (summer): Jackson Schneider, Colleen O’Donnell
