Join us

Students wishing to become involved in the EPL are expected to prepare and meet the following requirements:

  • Have taken ECON 220 and 320 (or equivalent) and demonstrate beyond-classroom experience or interest in quantitative skills using either R, Python, or another object-oriented language.
  • Have a GitHub account and are able to perform basic Git functions and processes (push/pull/merge) to work collaboratively
  • Be willing to actively collaborate on a daily or every-other-day basis via Slack with a project team
  • Be familiar with LaTeX and/or Overleaf
  • Have prepared a simple professional website

We accept students as rising Sophomores through to Seniors; to join, send an e-mail to the director with a one page description of your preparation and interest in joining the EPL.
