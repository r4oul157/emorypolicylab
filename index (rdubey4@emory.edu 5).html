Emory Policy Lab

The Emory Policy Lab is a place where students interested in quantitative analysis work on faculty-led, policy-relevant research. Students typically join the Lab for an academic year (two semesters) and work on small, structured teams to produce technical reports or research papers applying empirical analysis, machine learning, and other skills to real-world data.
